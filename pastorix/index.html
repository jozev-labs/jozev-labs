How It Works · Cómo funciona

Four layers working together for your herd.

01
Smart Collar
Each animal wears a lightweight GPS-connected collar with LoRa radio, sound emitters, and a mild audio-first cue system. Animals learn boundaries within days with minimal stress — the electrical element is a last-resort fallback calibrated well below any harm threshold.
Collar GPS con LoRa y señales de sonido. Los animales aprenden los límites en pocos días sin estrés.
02
Virtual Boundaries
Draw grazing zones on a digital map. Adjust or rotate boundaries in seconds from any device. No posts, no wire, no labour — just a tap to move an entire herd to a fresh paddock.
Dibuje y rote zonas de pastoreo en el mapa con un toque. Sin postes ni mano de obra.
03
LoRa Network
Solar-powered LoRa gateways relay collar data across thousands of hectares — no cellular infrastructure needed. Purpose-built for South America's remote ranching terrain and intermittent connectivity.
Gateways LoRa solares cubren miles de hectáreas sin red celular.
04
Farm Dashboard
Live herd location, grazing pressure heatmaps, soil health scores, boundary alerts, and productivity reports — all in one platform, accessible from any device, anywhere.
Dashboard en tiempo real: ubicación, alertas, métricas de productividad y salud del suelo.
🐄
Animal Welfare First · Bienestar animal primero Pastorix uses sound as its primary boundary signal — cattle learn to associate the audio cue with the fence line within a few days, similar to standard training. Electrical stimulation is a minimal, calibrated last-resort and is aligned with published virtual fence research on animal welfare. We do not compromise on this.

The Technology

Engineered for remote ranching conditions.

GPS + LoRa Collar
Ruggedised collar with multi-year battery, GPS accuracy to 3m, and LoRa range up to 15km in open terrain. Audio-first cueing system — cattle learn boundary behaviour quickly with minimal stress.
Solar LoRa Gateways
Self-powered gateways relay collar data without cellular infrastructure. One gateway covers thousands of hectares — no ongoing connectivity costs, purpose-built for remote South American terrain.
Farm Management Software
Draw zones, monitor herds live, review grazing history, export carbon reports, and receive instant alerts — from any device with internet, including low-bandwidth connections.
Soil & Productivity Analytics
Grazing intensity overlays correlate animal movement with pasture recovery, soil compaction, and vegetation health — making regenerative decisions measurable and bankable.
